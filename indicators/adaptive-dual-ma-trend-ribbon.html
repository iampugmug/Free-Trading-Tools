Adaptive Dual MA Trend Ribbon (Larsson-Style)

A dual-moving-average trend ribbon for TradingView using SMMA/EMA/SMA/WMA pairs with optional ATR neutral zones.

Open on TradingView

How Traders Recreate This Without the Indicator

Plot two smoothed moving averages: one with length 15 and one with length 29. When the faster MA is above the slower MA, bias is bullish; below, bearish.

Overview

This indicator packages a commonly used community setup into a clean ribbon with optional filters.
