Blended Net Liquidity Correlation

A macro tool that builds a blended net-liquidity model from FRED data and measures how strongly that liquidity has moved with price across multiple time windows. The indicator is free to use on TradingView; the script is protected.

Open on TradingView

How Traders Recreate This Without the Indicator

At its core, this tool combines publicly available macro liquidity series into a single framework. You can approximate similar views manually in TradingView without using this indicator.

One common approach is to use TradingView’s symbol math feature:

  1. Open the symbol search box.
  2. Enter a math expression combining series (example style: WALCL − RRP − TGA). You can add/subtract/divide/multiply symbols directly.
  3. Apply smoothing (like an SMA) to reduce noise.
  4. Compare the result to price and test different offsets or timeframes.

This indicator packages those steps into a cleaner workflow with component toggles, weighting controls, smoothing, and correlation windows—so you can explore the same concepts faster.

Overview

Many traders use liquidity-based models to understand broad macro conditions and their potential influence on price. While institutional-grade models often rely on proprietary inputs, the community frequently studies public FRED series—such as WALCL, RRP, TGA, and BTFP—to construct transparent net-liquidity frameworks that approximate capital movement within the financial system.

This script does not replicate or reverse-engineer any commercial macro indicator. Instead, it provides a flexible structure for exploring how different liquidity components interact, and how those liquidity dynamics correlate with market price across various time windows.

What the Script Does

1. Builds a customizable blended net-liquidity model

  • Includes WALCL, RRP, TGA, BTFP, ΔTOTLL, and ΔM2.
  • Allows turning each component on/off.
  • Provides adjustable weighting for WALCL vs. non-WALCL models.
  • Supports optional SMA smoothing of the liquidity series.
  • Supports time-offsetting liquidity to study lagging effects.

2. Runs multi-period price correlations

  • 30D, 60D, 90D, 180D, 1Y, 1.5Y, 2Y, and a custom-length window.
  • Displays all correlations in a compact table.
  • Automatically highlights the highest-correlation window.
