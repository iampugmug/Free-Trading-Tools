Blended Net Liquidity Correlation

This indicator visualizes a customizable net-liquidity model built from public FRED data and calculates correlation between liquidity and price across multiple time windows. The indicator is free to use; the script is protected.

Open on TradingView

Click the chart to open the indicator on TradingView.

How Traders Recreate This Without the Indicator

Traders can approximate similar views using TradingView symbol math and public macro series. Build expressions, smooth them, and compare against price.

  1. Combine series with math (example style: WALCL − RRP − TGA).
  2. Smooth with SMA to reduce noise.
  3. Compare against price and test different timeframes/offsets.

Overview

Many traders use liquidity-based models to understand broad macro conditions and their potential influence on price. The community frequently studies public FRED series—such as WALCL, RRP, TGA, and BTFP—to construct transparent net-liquidity frameworks.

What the Script Does

  • Builds a blended liquidity model from selectable components.
  • Allows toggles, smoothing, and WALCL weighting.
  • Runs multi-window correlation analysis and highlights the strongest window.

Why This Script Is Useful

Liquidity models can help frame broader market context. This tool makes it easier to explore which liquidity components matter most and whether liquidity has tracked price across different time windows.
