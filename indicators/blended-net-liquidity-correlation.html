Blended Net Liquidity Correlation

This indicator visualizes a customizable net liquidity model built from public FRED data and measures correlation between liquidity and price across multiple time windows. The indicator is free to use; the script is protected.

Open on TradingView

Click the chart to open the indicator on TradingView.

How Traders Recreate This Without the Indicator

You can approximate similar views manually in TradingView using symbol math with public macro series. Traders often build expressions and smooth them to study broad liquidity direction.

  1. Use TradingView symbol math to add/subtract series (example style: WALCL − RRP − TGA).
  2. Apply smoothing (SMA) to reduce noise.
  3. Compare against price and test different timeframes/offsets.

What the Script Does

  • Builds a blended net liquidity model from components like WALCL, RRP, TGA, BTFP, lending, and M2.
  • Allows turning components on/off and weighting models.
  • Computes correlation vs price across multiple windows and highlights the highest.
  • Plots liquidity against price for macro context.
