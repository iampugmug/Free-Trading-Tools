Hybrid Confluence (RSI, MFI, StochRSI) Two-Tier Momentum Framework

Many traders explore multi-oscillator hybrid confluence approaches that combine momentum and volume signals—most commonly RSI, Money Flow Index (MFI), and Stochastic RSI—to study stretched market conditions.

This script provides a transparent framework built exclusively from standard, well-documented indicators. The goal is to study hybrid momentum behavior without relying on proprietary logic or black-box tools.

Screenshot preview. Once published, this can link to the TradingView script page.

What the Script Does

  • Combines RSI (price momentum), MFI (volume-weighted momentum), and StochRSI (momentum relative to its own range).
  • Normalizes all components on a 0–100 scale for comparison and aggregation.
  • Creates a two-tier structure: 2-of-3 early pressure vs 3-of-3 extreme alignment.
  • Highlights persistence by increasing intensity when 3-of-3 conditions continue.

Why This Script Is Useful

  1. Requires multi-indicator agreement instead of isolated signals.
  2. Separates early pressure from extreme conditions.
  3. Visualizes persistence rather than assuming one bar marks the exact top/bottom.
  4. Designed as contextual information, not a standalone trading system.

How to Use

  1. Adjust RSI/MFI/StochRSI lengths to fit your timeframe.
  2. Watch 2-of-3 for early pressure.
  3. Watch 3-of-3 for extreme alignment.
  4. Note intensity increases as conditions persist.
  5. Combine with trend/structure/volume for decisions.

What This Script Is Not

  • Not a paid or proprietary recreation.
  • Not a predictive “signal system”.
  • Does not claim to identify exact tops or bottoms.

Not signals. Not alerts. Not shortcuts.

The goal is to document how indicators work — not to tell anyone when to trade.

If an indicator can’t be explained down to its underlying mechanics, it probably isn’t understood well enough to trade.
