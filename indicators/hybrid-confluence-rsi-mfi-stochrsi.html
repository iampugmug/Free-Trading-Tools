Hybrid Confluence (RSI, MFI, StochRSI)

Many traders explore multi-oscillator hybrid confluence approaches that combine momentum and volume signals — most commonly RSI, Money Flow Index (MFI), and Stochastic RSI — to study stretched market conditions.

This script provides a fully transparent two-tier momentum framework built exclusively from standard, well-documented technical indicators.

What the Script Does

  • Combines RSI, MFI, and StochRSI on a normalized scale
  • 2-of-3 confluence highlights building pressure
  • 3-of-3 confluence highlights extreme alignment
  • Visual intensity increases with persistence

Why This Script Is Useful

  • Filters noise via multi-indicator agreement
  • Separates early pressure from extremes
  • Shows sustained momentum behavior

Not signals. Not alerts. Not shortcuts.
