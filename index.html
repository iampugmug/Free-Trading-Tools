About Open Indicator Lab

Most trading indicator pages are built to sell you something. Open Indicator Lab is the opposite: it documents useful tools for traders in a clean, neutral format. The goal is to explain what an indicator actually does, when it tends to help, and where it can mislead you.

All tools here are open-source or transparently documented. Nothing on this site is financial advice, a signal service, or a guarantee of performance. Use these indicators as building blocks inside your own framework, alongside risk management and your preferred approach to price action.
