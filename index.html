About Open Indicator Lab

Most trading indicator pages are built to sell you something. Open Indicator Lab is the opposite: it documents useful tools for traders in a clean, neutral, “Investopedia-style” format. The focus is on understanding what an indicator does, when it tends to help, and where it can mislead you.

All tools here are open-source or transparently documented. Nothing on this site is financial advice, a signal service, or a promise of performance.
