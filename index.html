Indicator Docs: Adaptive Dual MA Trend Ribbon

Open on TradingView

Overview

The Adaptive Dual MA Trend Ribbon is a two-line moving-average ribbon designed to highlight bullish / bearish trend phases and neutral zones using configurable SMMA / EMA / SMA / WMA pairs plus an optional ATR-based filter. It is inspired by community “Larsson-style” trend ribbons but remains fully open-source and transparent.

Default Settings (recommended starting point)

SMMA “Larsson-style” preset

  • Source: HL2
  • MA Type: SMMA (RMA)
  • Fast Length: 15
  • Slow Length: 29
  • Use ATR Neutral Filter: ON
  • ATR Length: 60
  • ATR Multiplier: 0.3 – 0.5

EMA “React quicker” preset

  • Source: HL2 or Close
  • MA Type: EMA
  • Fast Length: 30
  • Slow Length: 60
  • Use ATR Neutral Filter: ON
  • ATR Length: 60
  • ATR Multiplier: 0.3

How to use it

  1. Apply the indicator and start with one of the presets above.
  2. Treat orange as bullish context, blue as bearish, and gray as neutral/chop.
  3. Use the ribbon as a context filter, not as a stand-alone buy/sell system.
  4. On higher timeframes (4H, 1D, 1W), consider slower MAs (e.g. EMA 50/100).
  5. On intraday setups, test shorter EMAs (e.g. 10/21) and a smaller ATR multiplier.

Things to keep in mind

  • All moving averages lag; the ribbon won’t catch exact tops or bottoms.
  • ATR-neutral zones help avoid chop but cannot remove all noise.
  • Always combine with risk management, position sizing, and stop placement.
