About Open Indicator Lab

Most trading indicator pages are built to sell you something or hide the logic behind buzzwords. Open Indicator Lab is the opposite: it documents useful tools for traders in a clean, neutral format, with a focus on clarity instead of hype.

The goal is to explain what an indicator actually does, where it tends to help, and where it can mislead you — so you can decide whether it belongs in your process. Part of that is giving traders free, well-documented alternatives to expensive “secret sauce” indicators that often boil down to transparent math or standard data.

Indicators here are free to use on TradingView. Many of them are published as protected scripts (the code itself isn’t exposed), but inputs, behavior, and use-cases are documented as openly as possible. Nothing on this site is financial advice, a signal service, or a guarantee of performance.
